Section5Molecular models¶ permalink

This is another dummy section. The html has been edited to illustrate what the inclusion of views of molecular models might look like.

The views of these models are also dependent on three.js.

A possible form for the XML tags:

<molecule>
	<source> molecule.pdb </source>
	<viewpoint> [100, 50, -75] </viewpoint>
	<description> 
		Words that describe the molecule.
	</description>
</molecule>
