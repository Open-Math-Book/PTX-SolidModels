To compute \(\gcd(a,b) = d\text{,}\), we are using repeated divisions to obtain a decreasing sequence of positive integers \(r_1 \gt r_2 \gt \cdots \gt r_n = d\text{;}\); that is, \begin{align*} b & = a q_1 + r_1\\ a & = r_1 q_2 + r_2\\ r_1 & = r_2 q_3 + r_3\\ & \vdots \\ r_{n - 2} & = r_{n - 1} q_{n} + r_{n}\\ r_{n - 1} & = r_n q_{n + 1}. \end{align*}
