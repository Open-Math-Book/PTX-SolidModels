Let \({\mathbb N}^0 = {\mathbb N} \cup \{ 0 \}\text{.}\). Ackermann's function is the function \(A :{\mathbb N}^0 \times {\mathbb N}^0 \rightarrow {\mathbb N}^0\) defined by the equations \begin{align*} A(0, y) & = y + 1,\\ A(x + 1, 0) & = A(x, 1),\\ A(x + 1, y + 1) & = A(x, A(x + 1, y)). \end{align*}
