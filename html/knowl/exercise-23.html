Define a function on the real numbers by \begin{equation*} f(x) = \frac{x + 1}{x - 1}. \end{equation*} What are the domain and range of \(f\text{?}\) What is the inverse of \(f\text{?}\) Compute \(f \circ f^{-1}\) and \(f^{-1} \circ f\text{.}\)

Hint

\(f^{-1}(x) = (x+1)/(x-1)\text{.}\)
