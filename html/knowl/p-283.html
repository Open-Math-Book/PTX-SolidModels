To find \(r\) and \(s\) such that \(ar + bs = d\text{,}\), we begin with this last equation and substitute results obtained from the previous equations: \begin{align*} d & = r_n\\ & = r_{n - 2} - r_{n - 1} q_n\\ & = r_{n - 2} - q_n( r_{n - 3} - q_{n - 1} r_{n - 2} )\\ & = -q_n r_{n - 3} + ( 1+ q_n q_{n-1} ) r_{n - 2} \\ & \vdots \\ & = ra + sb. \end{align*}
