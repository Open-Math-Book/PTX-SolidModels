First suppose that \(a^k=e\text{.}\). By the division algorithm, \(k = nq + r\) where \(0 \leq r \lt n\text{;}\); hence, \begin{equation*} e = a^k = a^{nq + r} = a^{nq} a^r = e a^r = a^r. \end{equation*} Since the smallest positive integer \(m\) such that \(a^m = e\) is \(n\text{,}\), \(r= 0\text{.}\).
