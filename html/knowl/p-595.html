If \(H = \{ 2^n : n \in {\mathbb Z} \}\text{,}\), then \(H\) is a subgroup of the multiplicative group of nonzero rational numbers, \({\mathbb Q}^*\text{.}\). If \(a = 2^m\) and \(b = 2^n\) are in \(H\text{,}\), then \(ab^{-1} = 2^m 2^{-n} = 2^{m-n}\) is also in \(H\text{.}\).
