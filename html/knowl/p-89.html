Sometimes it is the case that \(f \circ g= g \circ f\text{.}\). Let \(f(x) = x^3\) and \(g(x) = \sqrt[3]{x}\text{.}\). Then \begin{equation*} (f \circ g )(x) = f(g(x)) = f( \sqrt[3]{x}\, ) = (\sqrt[3]{x}\, )^3 = x \end{equation*} and \begin{equation*} (g \circ f )(x) = g(f(x)) = g( x^3) = \sqrt[3]{ x^3} = x. \end{equation*}
